Game Overview

Day Planner is a decision-based simulation game where players experience one full day. Every decision affects Stress, Energy, and Grades. The goal is to balance all three while dealing with random events and time-based choices.

---

Gameplay

The game is split into four phases of the day:

  • Morning
  • School
  • Afternoon
  • Night

Each phase gives you choices that affect your stats and future events.

---

Example Choices

Morning

Sleep in, Wake up early, Eat breakfast, Check phone, Exercise

School

Focus in class, Talk to friends, Skip homework, Study, Use phone

Afternoon

Study, Play games, Go outside, Spend time with friends, Nap

Night

Stay up late, Finish homework, Watch videos, Sleep early, Prepare tomorrow

---

Random Events

  • Surprise test at school
  • Friend invites you out
  • Extra homework assigned
  • Bad weather increases stress
  • Phone battery dies
  • Oversleeping

Energy
